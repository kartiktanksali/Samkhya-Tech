Who are we? Why are we here?

The most emerging industry in past few years has been the health sector of India. This sector is more and more trying to adapt the Technology in its industry. Samkhya Tech will be focusing on getting into the “Health Care Sector with respect to Information & Technology”. Samkhya Tech will be producing technology which will help the community of doctors and try to make their work easier.

Mission & Goal:

Samkhya Tech’s main mission is to bring the smart phone technology in the Health care industry, which in turn should help the community and in the end heal the people.

Primary Customers:

Our products will mainly be used by the MEDICOS. Usage of our technology will help them take decisions about the treatment and help the patient heal in a proper way.

Product Name:
